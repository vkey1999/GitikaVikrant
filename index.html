The Day-by-Day
Itinerary

1
Apr 23
Fly to Goa → Agonda — Settle In
Arrival · first beach sunset · seafood dinner
›
  • 1:00 PM
    Depart Mumbai & Fly to Goa
    Take afternoon flight — arrive Goa by 2:30 PM. Dabolim (GOI) is closer to Agonda (60 km, 1.5 hrs).
    ✈ IndiGo/Air India BOM→GOI
  • 3:30 PM
    Taxi to Agonda Beach
    Straight to Agonda. Check into your beach cottage — request sea-facing room. Options: White Sand Resort, Secret Garden Agonda.
    🚕 Pre-booked taxi ₹1,200–1,600
  • 5:30 PM
    First Walk on Agonda Beach
    Agonda is a 3 km crescent beach with almost no one. April = zero crowds. Walk its full length barefoot. This is your home.
  • 7:30 PM
    Sunset Seafood Dinner 💑
    Dersy Bar or Fatima's Corner on the beach. Fresh surmai, pomfret, Kingfisher. Watch the sun drop into the Arabian Sea. ₹600–1,000 for two.
2
Apr 24
Agonda Morning + Cabo de Rama Fort
Cliff fort · hidden Cola Beach · golden hour walk
›
  • 7:00 AM
    Agonda Beach Morning Swim
    Calm sea, warm water. Swim freely — Agonda has gentle waves. Coconut from beach vendor after.
  • 9:30 AM
    Rent a Scooter
    ₹400–500/day. Freedom for all day trips. Carries both of you easily.
    🛵 Resort or village rental
  • 10:30 AM
    Cabo de Rama Fort Hidden gem
    16th-century Portuguese fort on a sheer cliff over the Arabian Sea. Completely deserted in April. Walk through ruins, stand on the cliff edge. One of South Goa's most dramatic views.
    🛵 12 km, 25 min from Agonda
  • 1:00 PM
    Cola Beach — Secret Lagoon
    On the way back. A freshwater lagoon meeting the sea — swim in both. Requires a short walk down from the road. Very few visitors in April.
    🛵 4 km from Cabo de Rama
  • 5:00 PM
    Agonda Sunset + Turtle Watch
    April is Olive Ridley turtle nesting season. Walk the empty northern stretch — turtles nest here at night. Golden light, total silence.
3
Apr 25
Dolphin Boat Trip + Butterfly Beach
Private boat · Honeymoon Cove · dolphins · couples spa
›
  • 8:30 AM
    Private Boat — Coastal Discovery 💑 Most romantic morning
    Hire a private boat for 2 hrs from Agonda beach. Route: Agonda → Honeymoon Beach (tiny boat-only cove) → Butterfly Beach (inaccessible by road, shaped like a butterfly) → dolphin spotting. April morning = calm sea, dolphins almost guaranteed.
    ⛵ ₹1,200–1,800 for private boat
  • 12:30 PM
    Lunch at Agonda Shack
    Back on the beach. Lunch at a shack with sandy feet. ₹500–800 for two.
  • 4:00 PM
    Tandem Kayaking at Agonda
    Rent a tandem kayak (₹400–600/hr). Paddle along the shoreline at golden hour together.
  • 7:30 PM
    Couples Ayurvedic Massage 💑
    90-min couples session at an Agonda ayurvedic centre. After a day on the water, this is perfect. ₹1,500–2,500.
    🚶 5 min walk in Agonda village
4
Apr 26
Palolem Day Trip
Famous crescent bay · kayak to island · sunset from the bay
›
  • 9:00 AM
    Scooter to Palolem
    9 km from Agonda. Arrive early before the heat. Palolem's crescent bay is one of the most photogenic in South Goa.
    🛵 20 min scenic coastal ride
  • 10:00 AM
    Kayak to Canacona Island
    Paddle to the small island at the northern end of Palolem bay. Land on it and explore. ₹400–600/hr.
  • 1:00 PM
    Lunch at Palolem
    Magic Italy for wood-fired pizza, or Oceanic Restaurant for Goan fish curry. ₹700–1,200 for two.
  • 3:00 PM
    Patnem Beach
    2 km south of Palolem. Smaller, quieter, empty in April afternoons. Last swim before sunset.
    🛵 2 km from Palolem
  • 5:30 PM
    Palolem Sunset from the Bay
    Palolem faces west — sunset over the bay with the headlands silhouetted. Beer at a beach shack.
5
Apr 27
Lazy Agonda Day + Sunset Cruise
No agenda · Galgibaga turtles · candlelit beach dinner
›
  • 9:00 AM
    Absolutely Nothing
    Day 5. Wake up late. Long breakfast. Sit on Agonda beach. Read. Swim. Collect shells. Do nothing. This is the honeymoon version of a perfect day.
  • 4:30 PM
    Galgibaga Beach — Turtle Sanctuary
    15 km south. One of India's protected Olive Ridley nesting beaches. Completely deserted. Wild, long, empty. April = hatchlings sometimes emerging.
    🛵 30 min south of Agonda
  • 6:30 PM
    Sunset Boat Cruise 💑
    1-hr sunset cruise from Agonda. Watch the sun set from the water with a cold drink in hand. ₹500–800/person.
    ⛵ Operators on Agonda beach
  • 8:30 PM
    Private Candlelit Beach Dinner 💑 Most romantic night
    Many Agonda resorts arrange a private table set on the sand — fairy lights, candles, personal waiter. Book 1 day ahead. Last night at Agonda — make it count. ₹1,500–3,000.
6
Apr 28
Drive Agonda → Gokarna via NH66
Coastal highway · Karnataka border · Om Beach arrival
›
  • 9:00 AM
    Checkout Agonda
    Last swim. Pack up. Say goodbye to the turtles.
  • 10:00 AM
    Drive Agonda → Gokarna via NH66
    130 km on the coastal highway. Beautiful drive through Western Ghats foothills meeting the sea. Cashew orchards, rice paddies, sea glimpses. Stop at Karwar for lunch.
    🚗 Taxi ₹3,500–4,500 · 3–3.5 hrs
  • 1:30 PM
    Karnataka State Border (Majali)
    Carry Aadhaar. Brief check, no issues. Good chai stop here.
  • 3:30 PM
    Arrive Gokarna + Check In to Om Beach
    Stay on Om Beach — no cars can reach here, absolute silence. Options: SwaSwara (luxury), Om Beach Resort, Nirvana Café huts. Request an ocean-facing room.
    🚶 Short walk from taxi drop
  • 5:30 PM
    First Walk on Om Beach
    Walk to the headland — look back at the full Om-shaped bay. The view from above is the iconic Gokarna image. Water is cleaner than most Goa beaches.
  • 7:30 PM
    Dinner at Namaste Café
    The legendary Om Beach café. Prawn pizza and Goan fish thali. Stars overhead — zero light pollution. ₹500–800.
7
Apr 29
Gokarna Beach Trek
Om → Half Moon → Paradise · cliffside jungle trail
›
  • 8:00 AM
    Coastal Trek Begins Most scenic morning
    Start early before the heat builds. The coastal trek connects Om → Half Moon → Paradise Beach through dense forest with sea views from clifftops. 4 km total. April = zero other trekkers. Absolute silence except the waves below.
    🥾 Start from south end of Om Beach
  • 9:00 AM
    Half Moon Beach
    Crescent-shaped beach between two forested cliffs. A few huts, one café. One of the most beautiful isolated beaches in Karnataka. Swim — water is clear and calm in April.
    45 min trek from Om Beach
  • 10:30 AM
    Paradise Beach 💑 Most secluded
    The final, most secluded beach — accessible only by trek or boat. Bioluminescent plankton visible on new-moon nights. Swim at Dolphin Shanti Café. Completely private.
    30 min trek from Half Moon
  • 12:30 PM
    Boat Back to Om Beach
    Don't trek back in afternoon heat. Hire a boat from Paradise. ₹200–300/person. 15-min sail back along the coast.
    ⛵ ₹400–600 for two
  • 5:00 PM
    Kudle Beach Scooter Ride
    2 km north of Om Beach. Wide golden sand, cafes, very few people in April. Perfect sunset spot.
    🛵 10 min / walk 25 min over the hill
  • 7:00 PM
    Dinner at Mantra Café, Kudle
    Choco chip pancakes and seafood pasta with the sea view. ₹500–800 for two.
8
Apr 30
Gokarna Temple + Mirjan Fort
Ancient Shiva temple · jungle-covered fort · town walk
›
  • 8:30 AM
    Mahabaleshwar Temple
    One of South India's most sacred Shiva temples. Ancient, atmospheric, not touristy. A real temple experience. Dress modestly.
    🛺 Auto ₹100–150 from Om Beach
  • 10:00 AM
    Gokarna Town Walk
    Old temple town with ancient lanes, Brahmin houses, vibrant markets. Buy local honey, jaggery, coconut products.
  • 11:30 AM
    Mirjan Fort Hidden gem
    14th-century fort on the banks of the Aghanashini River — completely covered in jungle. Explore ruins inside a forest. Multiple gateways, bastions, moat. No entry fee. Almost never crowded. Incredibly romantic.
    🛵 21 km north · 35 min
  • 5:00 PM
    Last Gokarna Sunset at Om Beach 💑
    Sit on Om Beach as the sun sets one final time. No buildings, just the natural Om-shaped bay glowing gold.
  • 7:30 PM
    Final Gokarna Dinner at Moksha Café
    Seafood thali on Om Beach. Star-gazing after dinner. ₹500–900 for two.
9
May 1
Return to Goa — Palolem Night
Drive back · one night in the famous bay · Silent Noise
›
  • 9:00 AM
    Checkout Gokarna
    Last look at Om Beach. Pack up.
  • 9:30 AM
    Drive Gokarna → Palolem via NH66
    110 km, 3 hrs. Reverse of the journey down. Stop at Karwar for breakfast if needed.
    🚗 Taxi ₹3,000–4,000
  • 12:30 PM
    Check In to Palolem Beach Hut
    One night at Palolem. Ciaran's Camp (beach huts right on the sand) is the most atmospheric. You know Palolem from Day 4 — tonight you stay.
  • 5:00 PM
    Kayak to Canacona Island at Sunset
    Paddle to the island at the north end of the bay as the sun sets. The most beautiful angle of Palolem.
  • 9:00 PM
    Last Dinner at Palolem
    Magic Italy for the last wood-fired pizza of the trip. Or any favourite shack. Walk the beach strip at night one final time. ₹800–1,400.
10
May 2
Travel Home
Last beach sunrise · taxi to airport · fly to Mumbai
›
  • 7:00 AM
    Last Sunrise on Palolem Beach
    Walk to the beach before checkout. Watch the bay wake up. Cold coconut water. Take it in without rushing.
  • 8:30 AM
    Breakfast + Checkout
    Final Goan breakfast — poha, chai, or café omelette. Pack up.
  • 10:00 AM
    Taxi to Goa Airport
    Palolem to Dabolim: 75 km, 1.5 hrs.
    🚕 ₹1,800–2,200
  • 1:00 PM
    Fly Home to Mumbai 🌴
    Carry the smell of the sea, the taste of prawn curry, and the memory of Om Beach back with you.
